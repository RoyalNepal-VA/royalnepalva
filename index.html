Our Journey Since 2017

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings to becoming one of Washington's most celebrated Nepalese restaurants.

  • June 2017

    A Dream Takes Flight

    Opened our doors in the heart of Washington, bringing authentic Nepalese flavors and traditional family recipes to the community. Within months, we gained a loyal following for our commitment to authentic cuisine and warm hospitality.

  • March 2018

    First Washington Post Recognition

    Featured in The Washington Post's "Hidden Gems" section, our traditional momos and curry dishes received high praise. This recognition brought new waves of food enthusiasts eager to experience our authentic Nepalese cuisine.

  • September 2019

    Growth and Recognition

    Expanded our dining space and launched our specialty bar program. Received our second Washington Post feature highlighting our innovative Nepalese-inspired cocktails and achieved a milestone of 1000+ positive reviews with a 4.7/5 rating.

  • 2020-2021

    Resilience and Community

    Successfully adapted to challenging times by enhancing our takeout services and launching community support initiatives. Provided over 5,000 meals to frontline workers and maintained our high service standards, earning our third Washington Post feature for community engagement.

  • 2022-Present

    Celebrating Excellence

    Achieved a remarkable milestone of 2000+ reviews maintaining our 4.7/5 rating. Recognized as one of DC's top ethnic restaurants, we continue to serve our community with authentic Nepalese cuisine while innovating our menu and guest experience.
